1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use mobile apps published by developer TactiQwl (“Developer”, “we”, “us”) on Google Play, RuStore, and other app stores. It applies to all such apps unless a specific listing points to a different privacy policy URL.

2. What data we process

2.1. As a rule, the Developer does not create accounts, does not require registration, and does not ask for name, email, phone, precise location, or payment details inside the app. If that changes, this Policy will be updated or a separate policy URL will be used.

2.2. Progress, settings, and saves are usually stored locally on your device, are not sent to the Developer’s servers, and are removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

2.3. INTERNET permission may be used (ads, content, WebView). Camera, microphone, contacts, location, etc. are requested only if a specific app needs them.

2.4. Advertising. Some builds may contain ads (including Yandex Mobile Ads or other partners); others may ship without ads. If ads are enabled, the partner SDK may process Advertising ID, device info, IP address, and impression/click data under the partner’s policy (for Yandex: yandex.com/legal/confidential). The Developer does not sell personal data and does not intentionally share local progress with ad networks. You can limit ad personalization in device settings.

2.5. If an app has no separate analytics SDK and no Developer telemetry server, the Developer does not collect such analytics directly. Stores may collect statistics under their own policies.

3. Purposes of processing

  • operating the app and keeping progress / settings on the device;
  • showing ads and measuring performance (if ads are enabled);
  • complying with store requirements and applicable law;
  • responding to user contact requests.

4. Sharing with third parties

  • ad partners — ad-enabled builds only;
  • app distribution platforms (stores);
  • public authorities — when legally required.

5. Retention and security

Local data remains on your device until uninstall or clearing app data. Network traffic is protected by the OS and relevant services (typically HTTPS).

6. Children

Age audience follows the rating on the store listing. The Developer does not knowingly collect children’s personal data. Contact: tactiqwl@gmail.com.

7. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request information, correction, or deletion of data the Developer actually holds (e.g. support emails), and limit personalized ads in device settings. Local data is usually deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling. Requests: tactiqwl@gmail.com.

8–9. Third-party links and changes

Third-party sites follow their own policies. The current Policy stays at this URL; the update date is shown at the top.
